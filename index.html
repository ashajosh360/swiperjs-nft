Community

Hello everyone! Along with a team of photographers, we are here to create a community never seen in the Solana NFT space before. SolPics will build a very desired bridge, a bridge that will connect 3D Art with the beauty of Photography. As most of the collections in the space are based on it we decided to connect the strength of this type of art with the most frequently used tool to capture the moment. This project aims not only to the Solana community, but also to all of those still to come. To all of the photographers out there looking for a community with the opportunity to grow, share their work, and share their story. To those who experience the moment by looking at a photograph and to those who experience the love of the moment. To everyone, because photography is part of everything, it is nature as a whole. The 2,222 NFTs that we are going to release in the collection are going to be here to stay. Through it, valuable airdrops will be made for the holders, and overtime announcements will be made in which our different stages of the project will be explained through the roadmap. By having a SolPic, you just don't have a photo, you have art, beauty, nature, humanity, and love, all-in-one. Just imagine, that in the near future, you can display one of these NFTs in your house, studio, office or who knows, even a museum. We are revolutionizing the photography world. We came here to stay, welcome to the future!

Our way

RoadMap

October 2021

Two photographers, since January 2021 dedicating their time professionally to a passion, photography, come up with this crazy idea… SolPics. The first ever NFT photography collection in Solana, and with it a beautiful community for eternity. They decided to make something big for the photography community, something that will last forever.

November 2021

As photographers, we have always been amazed by different moments in history. Since the day that photography was created, lots of remarks have been saved in print art. Today, in the digital moment, we see how a new space is being created for all artists around the globe, the NFT world. SolPics, since its creation, it is intended to target all ages, nationalities, and people. The main purpose is to build a bridge from 3D art to Photography in the Solana NFT space, to expand the beauty of capturing the moment into a digital and accessible community for everyone. During this early stage, we will set-up all of our key resources and our art will be exposed. We will be filling up with news about the launch and build up the beginning of our community in Discord. Those who get in before the minting will have the opportunity of being benefited. While doing all of this, our team will process the foundations for not only short-term, but also mid-term and long-term. Yes, we are staying here forever. For you, creators.

December 2021

The launch of SolPics will be this month. After everything is minted, our project will reach one of the most important moments, each one who holds 4 or more of our NFT´s will be sent a special airdrop of the original picture taken. Holding one of our pictures means that you are part of this wonderful community that has been created for the purpose of bridging the gap between photography and 3D art. The magic part will be that after the minting is done, the photographers will reveal themselves and they will continue actively participating in discussions in discord with all of the community

January 2022

2022 will be the start of a new era in the Solana world. Our collection will be the first NFT project to fully integrate a new branch in the art world. But first we will have to focus on building this community into a creative hub where all photographers all over the world can provide their creative value in our upcoming projects and plans on our platform. The team will be constantly active in discord and twitter, organizing contests, scheduling voice calls and constantly supporting everyone into their passion.

February 2022

By then, the community will have a strong base, and everything will have just started. The team will send the original photos to the biggest holders via ordinary mail, no matter in what part of the world they are situated. This will be literally the first NFT photography collection in doing this and it will give it a lot more value.

Long Term...

The whole community that SolPics is creating will not only be influential to a lot of people, but it will also be a tool for young, and not that young photographers that want to enter the NFT space. This will be the beginning of something revolutionary, something that will make everyone think different about photography.

FAQ

Frequency Asked Questions

  • What are SolPics?

    Solpics is the FIRST collection of 2,222 unique private photographs stored in the Solana Blockchain. The main idea of this project is to combine the NFT´s 3D art with street photography from various cities in the world.

  • What is the total supply of SolPics?

    There will be 2,222 unique and captured photographs that are immutable on the Solana blockchain.

  • What is the cost of minting a SolPic?

    The minting price will be announced soon!

  • How many attributes/rarity?

    The rareness of each NFT will depend on the monkey and on the photography style. In regards to the monkey, we differentiate by type of monkey, skin and position. When speaking about the photography style, we decided to categorise them by street, nature, night and interior. Please check the rarity chart for more in depth clarification.

  • When is mint day?

    The mint day will be announced very soon, for now, we can say that around the first or second week of December 2021 .

  • Where can you mint your SolPics?

    The ONLY place to mint a solpic will be on our website, please make sure when you are minting, that the URL is correct. We would hate for you to get scammed.

  • What wallets can I use?

    You will be able to mint with phantom, sollet and solflare. We have created a medium post to teach you how to use phantom and mint on the big day!

  • Is there a wallet limit for minting solmushies?

    There is no max limit on the number of solpics you can mint in one wallet, but there will be a maximum of one photo per 1 tx.

  • Will there be an aftermarket?

    Yes, we are weighing out our options of what marketplaces we will be listed after launch. For the moment we are talking with Solanart, Digital Eyes, Magic Eden and Alpha.art.

  • Is there a roadmap?

    Yes! We have a section on this website!
